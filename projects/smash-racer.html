Smash Racer

Tech Stack: Unity, C#

Link to Windows Build: Smash Racer

I had two weeks to personally create this game in my class, and the only requirement was that it had to have a unique mechanic. In this case, my unique mechanic was a sideways dash that could destroy other cars in a never-ending racing game. It's a fairly straightforward game, and it was designed to feel very fast and out of control by the time you die.

Throughout the two weeks, I only had one time of outside feedback from my class, which was in the middle of the two weeks. From that feedback, I had to iterate on my design and improve the game and interestingness of the mechanic.

Over the creation of this game, I challenged myself by using blender to create all the 3D models, and I tried my hand at most of the pixel art in the game (although much of it was created by my friend, Gary O'Brien).
