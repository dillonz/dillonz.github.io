Flickernaut

Tech Stack: Unity, C#

Flickernaut was my Senior Capstone Project at University of Michigan. I worked closely with 3 other passionate students in the class over ~2 months to design, program, and iterate through this short video game. After the class, we enjoyed working with each other so much on it that we continued to develop it until we all got full time jobs and life got in the way. During the class, I worked for ~30-40 hours each week on the project.

Some key contributions by me:

  • All music was composed by me as well as the sound design. I created handlers to keep the two dimensions of music on the same measure always to create a seamless and immersive soundtrack
  • The initial ideation of having two dimensions, one dark, one light, and giving the player ways to freely move between them while they affected eachother
  • Creation of the torch lighting and movement between the light and dark dimension, as well as the restriction in certain situation of said dimensions
  • Many other bug fixes, enhancements, and ideas that were worked on either individually or as a team.
